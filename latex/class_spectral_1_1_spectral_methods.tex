A static method that computes the differentiation matrices D1, D2, ..., D\+M on Chebyshev nodes. ~\newline
The code implements two strategies for enhanced accuracy suggested by W.\+Don and S.\+Solomonoff in S\+I\+A\+M J.\+Sci.\+Comp.\+Vol. 6, pp. 1253--1268 (1994). The two strategies are(a) the use of trigonometric identities to avoid the computation of differences x(k) -\/ x(j) and(b) the use of the \char`\"{}flipping trick\char`\"{} which is necessary since sin t can be computed to high relative precision when t is small whereas sin(pi -\/ t) cannot. ~\newline
 J.\+A.\+C.\+Weideman, S.\+C.\+Reddy 1998.
